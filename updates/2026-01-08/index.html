New year, new research

Hoi, hou gaat het? I've been learning een beetje Nederlands. I am grateful that 2025 has been such a great year for me personally and professionally.

I participated in a bunch of international conferences to promote my work and learn other people's works. I have had a great time doing an R&D internship at WOTA as well!

Lately, I am doing an optimization project for Ba122 synthesis. It is a project I've been looking forward to since my first year of PhD, so I'm very excited that it is finally starting! The preliminary results seem promising, I hope this would be beneficial for the community.

It is a short update, but I am wishing you a wholesome 2026 :)
