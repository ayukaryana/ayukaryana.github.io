Hi, I'm Ayu.

I'm a second-year PhD student at Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology, working on iron-based superconductors. My research focuses on enhancing critical current density through grain boundary engineering and flux pinning optimization.

Supported by the JST SPRING fellowship, I've had the privilege of collaborating with groups at Oxford and NIMS, and presenting at conferences across Europe, Asia, and North America.

Beyond the lab, I'm drawn to where materials science meets computation, how machine learning can accelerate the search for better functional materials.
